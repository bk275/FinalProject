Power metal is a sub-genre of heavy metal that combines high vocals, piano or keyboards, epic lyrics, fantastical costumes, and a focus on a fast, light, uplifting sound. This website will go over the history of power metal, it's different variants, and my personal recommendations for bands as well. There will also be pages for individual bands as well.
