Sabaton

Sabaton is one my favorite power metal bands. They sing about military history, like famous battles, battalions, or people. Their recent Great War album is an entire album singing about World War I. It covers big names like the Red Baron or the battle at Verdun while also covering lesser known instances like the Attack of the Dead Men or Francis Pegahmagabow. They have a lot of fun with it and dress in pseudo-military themed garb and have tanks at their shows.

Gloryhammer

Gloryhammer is another of my favorite power metal bands, especially because they sing about a fantasy world. Their only 3 albums so far talk about the legend of a line of kings called the McFifes that battle an evil wizard named Zargothrax throughout the centuries. Each of the band members dress up in different costumes as well because they all play different characters within their stories.

Helloween

Helloween is widely known as the pioneer of European power metal, and they really show it. Their fun and party-like lyrics reflect a certain camp that is expected of the genre. My previous picks have very specific styles but Helloween have gone in every direction possible over the course of their career. There's something in their discography for everyone and they are always a joy to listen to.
